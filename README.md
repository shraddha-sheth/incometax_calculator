## üìñ How to Use

1. **Personal Information**: Select your age bracket and residential status
2. **Income from Salary**: Enter your salary components, HRA, and allowances
3. **House Property**: Add rental income and home loan interest if applicable
4. **Other Income Sources**: Include capital gains, business income, and interest
5. **Deductions**: Enter investments and expenses eligible for deductions (Old Regime only)
6. **View Results**: Compare Old vs New regime side-by-side
7. **Choose Wisely**: Select the regime that minimizes your tax liability
